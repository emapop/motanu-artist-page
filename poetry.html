Fuck Horoscope Signs

I won’t tell you my horoscope sign
just for you to label me
I won’t tell you my horoscope sign
so you can have the impression you know who I am, how I act

our first conversation won't tell you what my personality is like
you don't know who I am or what I’ve been through only by reading me

just because I let the wrong people in once doesn’t mean it will happen again
I shared my personal life, my thoughts, my heart with the wrong people who took advantage of my
kindness and my soul.

I don’t need a therapist I need love

I was born in the wrong era
maybe I lived other lives but only now am I writing a book
only now am I awake
only now am I conscious
the kid that grew up watching soap operas and commercials with the coca cola truck, listened to
manele that were old school, like Adrian Minune, grew up with skinned knees playing football by

concrete jungle I’ve been an artist since I was little

Death cake

(Coliva, romanian dessert served at funerals)

I refused to eat your death cake out of respect.
when I came out of my mom’s vagina I couldn’t talk yet
but if I had my words with me, I would’ve told her
madrelow, you’re a poetess, you just gave birth to a poem
a poem worthy of national and international acclaim
because nobody is more special than I am, except you
and from poem I became a heart that walks on two legs
and after that, I learned how to avoid pits and human trash and to spit, poetically
I became a poet, and from poet I’ll turn into stardust, which will become sand on mars, and after that,
I’ll become a comet that’ll crash once more into the planet of patriarchy, violence, crime, power,
abusers, non-artists and fakers.
